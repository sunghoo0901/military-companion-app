# Product Specification

> **This document defines the core structure, experience, and functionality of Military Companion. It serves as the blueprint for the product and should guide design, engineering, and future product decisions.**

---

# Product Overview

Military Companion is a mobile-first platform that supports Korean soldiers throughout their military journey.

The application combines personalized planning, military knowledge, financial guidance, career preparation, and AI assistance into one seamless experience.

Rather than functioning as a collection of independent tools, Military Companion should feel like one continuous companion that grows alongside the soldier.

---

# Core Product Experience

Military Companion is organized around **the soldier's journey**, not around isolated features.

Every screen should help the soldier answer one of three questions:

* Where am I?
* What should I do next?
* What opportunities should I know about?

---

# Primary Navigation

The application consists of six primary sections.

## 1. Home

### Purpose

Provide an overview of the soldier's current military journey.

### Key Components

* Military journey progress
* Upcoming milestones
* Recommended actions
* Leave summary
* Promotion progress
* Recent notifications

---

## 2. Journey

### Purpose

Help soldiers visualize their military service from enlistment to discharge.

### Key Components

* Timeline
* Calendar
* Milestones
* Promotions
* Training events
* Important dates

---

## 3. Benefits

### Purpose

Help soldiers discover opportunities available to them.

### Categories

* Financial
* Banking
* Transportation
* Restaurants
* Entertainment
* Education
* Travel
* Government programs
* Military-exclusive offers

Benefits should be personalized whenever possible.

---

## 4. AI Companion

### Purpose

Provide trusted, conversational guidance.

The AI should:

* Explain military regulations.
* Answer questions.
* Recommend next actions.
* Personalize responses.
* Distinguish verified information from assumptions.

The AI should function as a knowledgeable senior—not as a generic chatbot.

---

## 5. Career

### Purpose

Prepare soldiers for life after discharge.

### Key Components

* Certifications
* Resume guidance
* Internship opportunities
* Job opportunities
* Educational resources
* Career roadmap

---

## 6. Profile

### Purpose

Manage the user's personal information and application preferences.

### Includes

* Military information
* Unit information
* Goals
* Notification settings
* Privacy settings
* Account management

---

# Personalization

The application should personalize the experience using:

* Enlistment date
* Branch
* Rank
* Unit
* Service duration
* Qualifications
* Goals

No two users should have an identical experience.

---

# AI Integration

Artificial intelligence is an interface—not the product itself.

The AI should:

* Guide.
* Explain.
* Recommend.
* Summarize.
* Personalize.

The AI should never replace verified military information.

Instead, it should help soldiers understand and apply it.

---

# Design Principles

The interface should feel:

* Calm
* Professional
* Friendly
* Trustworthy
* Encouraging

Information should be presented progressively rather than overwhelming the user.

---

# User Journey

Every time a soldier opens Military Companion, the application should immediately answer:

1. Where am I in my military journey?
2. What should I focus on today?
3. What opportunities am I missing?

These questions should shape every design decision.

---

# Product Success

Military Companion succeeds when soldiers feel:

* Supported
* Prepared
* In control
* Confident

Ultimately, the application should help every soldier leave military service knowing they made the most of their time.

---

# Future Expansion

Potential future capabilities include:

* Family portal
* Veteran mode
* Official Ministry integration
* Administrative dashboard
* Community features (if validated)
* Advanced AI planning

Future additions should strengthen the soldier's journey rather than distract from it.
