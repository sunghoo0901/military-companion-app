# 🔴 P0 — MVP Features

---

## Personalized Military Journey Dashboard

**Status:** 📝 Planned

**Problem**

Soldiers struggle to understand where they are in their military journey and what comes next.

**Value**

Provides a personalized dashboard showing milestones, progress, and upcoming actions.

---

## Military Timeline

**Status:** 📝 Planned

**Problem**

Soldiers often create their own spreadsheets to plan military service.

**Value**

Displays important dates, milestones, promotions, and discharge progress.

---

## Leave & Vacation Planner

**Status:** 📝 Planned

**Problem**

Leave calculations are confusing and vary by unit.

**Value**

Helps soldiers estimate, track, and plan leave.

---

## Promotion Tracker

**Status:** 📝 Planned

**Problem**

Promotion requirements are difficult to understand.

**Value**

Tracks requirements and reminds soldiers of upcoming tasks.

---

## Benefits Hub

**Status:** 📝 Planned

**Problem**

Many soldiers never discover available benefits.

**Value**

Personalized list of financial, travel, education, and lifestyle benefits.

---

## AI Military Assistant

**Status:** 📝 Planned

**Problem**

Soldiers don't always know where to find trustworthy information.

**Value**

Answers questions, explains regulations, and guides users.

# Parking Lot

Ideas that are interesting but not currently prioritized.

* Community forums
* Anonymous Q&A
* Marketplace
* Military marketplace for used items
* Social networking
* Gamification beyond simple achievements

These ideas are intentionally deferred until the core mission has been validated.

---

# Feature Acceptance Rule

A feature should only move into active development if:

* It solves a validated user problem.
* It aligns with our mission.
* It fits the current product roadmap.
* It delivers meaningful value to soldiers.

If a feature fails these criteria, it should remain in the backlog or be rejected.

---

# Product Philosophy Reminder

We do not build features because they are exciting.

We build features because they make military life easier, clearer, or more rewarding.
