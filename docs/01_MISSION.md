# Military Companion Mission

> **Help every Korean soldier get the most out of military service.**

---

# Our Mission

Military Companion exists to empower every Korean soldier with the knowledge, tools, and guidance needed to maximize their military experience.

Military service is a significant chapter in a person's life. While every soldier shares the same obligation to serve, their experiences can differ dramatically depending on the information, opportunities, and guidance they receive.

Our mission is to ensure that no soldier misses an opportunity simply because they didn't know it existed.

---

# What We Believe

We believe that military service should not be a period of uncertainty.

Every soldier deserves access to accurate information, thoughtful planning tools, financial guidance, career resources, and personalized support throughout their service.

Technology should reduce uncertainty—not create more of it.

---

# Our Purpose

Military Companion helps soldiers:

* Understand military regulations and processes.
* Plan their service with confidence.
* Discover benefits and opportunities available to them.
* Prepare financially during military service.
* Build a stronger foundation for life after discharge.
* Make informed decisions at every stage of their military journey.

---

# Our Commitment

We are committed to building a platform that is:

* Accurate before fast.
* Helpful before impressive.
* Simple before complicated.
* Trusted before popular.

Every feature we build should improve the lives of soldiers in a meaningful way.

---

# Success Criteria

We succeed when soldiers:

* Feel less confused.
* Feel more prepared.
* Miss fewer opportunities.
* Spend less time searching for information.
* Leave military service better prepared than when they entered.

---

# Our Guiding Question

Whenever we consider building a new feature, we ask one question:

> **Will this genuinely help soldiers make the most of their military service?**

If the answer is no, we don't build it.

This principle will guide every product decision we make.
