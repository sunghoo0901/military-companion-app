# Product Requirements

> **This document defines the functional requirements for the first version (MVP) of Military Companion.**

---

# Purpose

The goal of the MVP is to solve the most common and highest-impact problems faced by Korean soldiers during active military service.

The MVP should prove that Military Companion provides meaningful value before expanding into additional features or user groups.

---

# Target User

The MVP is designed primarily for:

* Active-duty Republic of Korea soldiers.

Future versions may expand to other branches, reserve personnel, and pre-enlistment users.

---

# Core Product Goals

The MVP must help soldiers:

* Understand where they are in their military journey.
* Plan upcoming milestones.
* Discover benefits and opportunities.
* Understand promotion requirements.
* Plan leave and vacation.
* Prepare financially.
* Prepare for life after discharge.

---

# Functional Requirements

## 1. Personalized Military Journey

The application shall provide a personalized timeline based on:

* Enlistment date
* Branch
* Unit (when applicable)
* Rank
* Expected discharge date

The timeline should display important milestones throughout military service.

---

## 2. Leave & Vacation Planning

The application shall:

* Track available leave.
* Estimate future leave accrual.
* Display planned leave.
* Help users understand how leave is calculated.
* Support unit-specific adjustments where applicable.

---

## 3. Promotion Guidance

The application shall:

* Display promotion requirements.
* Track completed requirements.
* Remind users of upcoming promotion-related tasks.
* Explain promotion processes in simple language.

---

## 4. Benefits Hub

The application shall provide verified information about:

* Military discounts
* Financial programs
* Government support
* Bank benefits
* Educational opportunities
* Travel and leisure benefits

Whenever possible, benefits should be personalized to the user.

---

## 5. Financial Planning

The application shall help soldiers:

* Learn about military savings programs.
* Track financial goals.
* Understand available financial benefits.
* Prepare for post-service financial planning.

---

## 6. Career Preparation

The application shall provide guidance on:

* Certifications
* Resume preparation
* Job opportunities
* Internship information
* Educational pathways
* Discharge preparation

---

## 7. AI Assistant

The application shall include an AI assistant capable of:

* Answering military-related questions.
* Explaining regulations in plain language.
* Helping users find relevant information.
* Directing users to appropriate resources.

The AI should clearly distinguish verified information from general guidance.

---

# Non-Functional Requirements

The MVP should be:

* Easy to use.
* Fast.
* Reliable.
* Mobile-friendly.
* Accessible.
* Easy to maintain.

---

# MVP Success Criteria

The MVP is considered successful if users report that it:

* Saved them time.
* Reduced uncertainty.
* Helped them discover new opportunities.
* Improved planning.
* Made military life easier.

---

# Out of Scope

The MVP will **not** initially include:

* Social networking features.
* User-generated forums.
* Messaging between soldiers.
* Entertainment features.
* Features unrelated to military service.

These may be considered in future versions after validating the core product.

---

# Guiding Principle

Every feature included in the MVP must answer one question:

> **Does this make military service easier, clearer, or more rewarding for soldiers?**

If the answer is no, it does not belong in Version 1.
