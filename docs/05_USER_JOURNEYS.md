# The Conversation Never Ends

Military Companion should not treat onboarding as a setup process.

Instead, onboarding begins a conversation that continues throughout military service.

Examples:

First Day

"Welcome."

↓

Early Service

"Let's get to know you."

↓

Several Months Later

"I noticed you've been exploring certifications."

↓

Mid Service

"You're halfway through your service."

↓

Before Discharge

"Let's prepare for what's next."

↓

After Discharge (Future)

"Congratulations. What's your next goal?"

Every interaction should build upon previous conversations.

Military Companion grows alongside the soldier.

The relationship should become stronger over time.
